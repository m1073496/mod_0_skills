Class: Food


Attributes:

nameOfDish (string)
glutenFree (boolean)
numOfSides (integer)
servedHot (boolean)


Methods:

orderFood (this will retrieve and log the nameOfDish of a menu item)
addGluten (adds gluten to a dish, this may alter the boolean value of glutenFree)
changeMenu (this will add 1 side dish to the numOfSides (number of sides) for a certain dish)
serveCold (this will make a dish come out cold and may alter the servedHot boolean value)
